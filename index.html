Our Story

A Lighthouse of Hope Since 1941

For over 80 years, Britton Baptist Church has been shining God's light in North Oklahoma City. What began as a small congregation on Britton Road has grown into a vibrant, faith-driven community committed to serving people from all walks of life.

We believe that God is still at work — in our church, in our neighborhood, and in the lives of everyone who walks through our doors. Whether you're searching for answers, looking for community, or deepening your faith, you're welcome here.

Walk in the light, as He is in the light, and we have fellowship with one another. 1 John 1:7

Our doors are open to everyone. Come as you are and discover what it means to walk in the light together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Basketball Court Rental FAQ

Where can I rent an indoor basketball court in Oklahoma City?
Britton Baptist Church at 1141 W Britton Rd in North Oklahoma City offers a full-size indoor basketball court for hourly rental. The hardwood court features regulation hoops, climate control, free parking, and optional live streaming through Pickup.Earth. Book online through our Sked.Earth widget.
How do I book a basketball court at Britton Baptist Church?
Use the Sked.Earth booking widget on this page. Select your date and time, pay securely online, and receive instant confirmation. You can also call (405) 842-3511 for assistance or custom event inquiries.
Do I need to be a church member to rent the court?
No. The basketball court is open to everyone in the Oklahoma City community. Church membership is not required. Everyone is welcome at Britton Baptist.
Is there pickup basketball near me in OKC?
Yes! Britton Baptist Church hosts pickup basketball sessions and open gym times in North Oklahoma City. Book a session through Sked.Earth or contact us for the current open gym schedule.
Can I live stream my basketball game?
Yes. Our court features professional cameras powered by Pickup.Earth. Games can be live streamed in HD, with footage archived for 30 days and available for download. Share highlights with friends and family anywhere in the world.
Can I rent the gym for events other than basketball?
Absolutely. The gym is available for volleyball, futsal, birthday parties, youth sports events, church group activities, corporate team-building, and more. Contact us for custom event pricing and availability.
Where is Britton Baptist Church located?
We're at 1141 W Britton Rd, Oklahoma City, OK 73114 — in North OKC near the intersections of Britton Rd, May Ave, and I-235. Free parking is available on-site. We're easily accessible from Edmond, The Village, Nichols Hills, and the greater OKC metro area.
What are the church service times at Britton Baptist?
Sunday School at 9:30 AM, Morning Worship at 10:50 AM, Evening Worship at 6:00 PM. Wednesday: Fellowship Meal at 5:00 PM, Children & Youth at 6:00 PM, Midweek Service at 6:15 PM. Everyone is welcome.
